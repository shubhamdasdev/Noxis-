## Outcome

A new user goes from install to receiving their first personalized, opinionated recommendation in under 3 minutes — through a hybrid flow of polished visual screens and conversational onboarding inside chat. By the end, Noxis knows the user's tone preference, primary focus area, and enough baseline context to deliver real value from the first interaction.
