## Outcome

The user gets instant, personalized advice through a premium chat interface — text and image. Sending a message or photo and getting an opinionated, specific response feels fast, private, and effortless. The chat is the primary surface where Noxis delivers value, extracts memory, and builds the relationship over time. Image input (outfit checks, meal photos, body progress) works from a center-bar camera button with zero friction.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Messages sent per active user per day | 0 (new product) | ≥ 3 by month 3 |
| Image-based interactions (outfit/meal/body checks) per week across all users | 0 | 2,000 by month 3 |
