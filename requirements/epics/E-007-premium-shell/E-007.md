## Outcome

The app feels premium, private, and fast from first launch. A black + glass aesthetic with no generic iOS chrome creates the visual identity. The navigation structure — Instagram-style bottom tab bar (Chat, Modules, Camera, Daily, Profile) combined with a ChatGPT-style side panel — gives the user fast, intuitive access to every surface without clutter. The camera button sits center-bar for zero-friction image capture. The entire shell communicates: this is a high-end tool built for a specific purpose.
