## Outcome

Every morning starts with a tailored briefing that drives action — not passive consumption. Noxis delivers a timezone-aware daily brief with specific insights grounded in real progress, max 3 action items pulled from the user's goals and recent patterns, and optional curated content. The daily brief makes Noxis feel less like a tool and more like a personal operating system. When the user opens the app in the morning, they see their brief first.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
