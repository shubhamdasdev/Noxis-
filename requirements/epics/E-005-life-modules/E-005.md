## Outcome

The user's life across wardrobe, gym, food, spending, and routines is organized into connected, visible, and actionable modules — not buried in chat history. Data flows both ways: Noxis extracts structured information from chat conversations and writes it to modules automatically, while the user can also manually add, edit, and review items directly in each module. Each module surfaces patterns, tracks progress, and feeds context back into Noxis's responses.
